Smart Home

Designed to automate different services to a same network using Internet access. This gives the customer the opportunity to manage or operate inside and outside their home or business; Many implementations do not only seek to systematize a device, but the optimization of energy consumption and technology management; for this reason the places that have this type of systems are also known as smart homes or smart business.

These are some of the services that we provide for smart houses or smart businesses:


Automation of the electrical system: Its main goal is the intelligent use of electrical devices, improving performance and therefore consumption, resulting in a lower bill of the electric service.

Illumination Comfort: Design to simplify the turning on/off the lights, the power supply of each of the electricity connectors or telephone intercoms integration and connectivity via the Internet for easy handling.

Security: It complements a security system by implementing additional functions that would avoid physical presence, such as closing blinds, door locks, installation of gas sensors for kitchens, water pressure sensors which determine the presence of leaks and simulation presence, etc.

Communications: Design to provide additional access to the system control internally and externally, controlling phones and video porters and allowing the implementation of consumption reports.
