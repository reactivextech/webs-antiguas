Intrusion Detection

Organizations today are in constant search of automated controls that allow them to monitor, control and protect internal and external factors that interact with them. The intrusion detection systems provide security and control to the employs and none employees that come in contact with the facilities. These are some of the security systems we provide:

CCTV: Close circuit television Systems are designed to monitor in audio and video format any incident that could had happen, offering the option to keep records stored locally or on a cloud. It is also important to note that staff behavior tends to remain more even within internal company policies when they are monitored with CCTV systems.

ALARMS: Design to alert any irregular situations that could end in great losses; these systems every day are more computerized and the ability to interact with other systems is growing every day. Among its accessories, we can use motion sensors, smoke sensor, pressure sensors, etc.

Biometric reader: Also known as fingerprint reader, used as an identifier to which you can program many functions, such as providing a physical access to an organization, keep records of employees attendance, authorize computer access, etc. They are also able to integrate additional functions to other systems.
